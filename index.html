ABOUT US

Welcome to CC KUBB TEAM, established 2008 Based in Stockholm, Sweden! We are a dedicated group of players who love the game of kubb. We've been out competing at the World and European Championships. Our recent win at the Norrtälje Cup (January 2026) and #10 national ranking reflect our recent progression in the sport.


For us, kubb is a passion we share as a team. We see every match as a chance to improve and enjoy competing together.
