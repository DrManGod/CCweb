ABOUT US

Welcome to CC KUBB TEAM based in Stockholm, Sweden! Since 2008, we've been toppling kubbs on the field with precision throws, tactical genius, and unbreakable team spirit. Our squad has competed in world and european championship tournaments, as well as regional competitions, bringing home medals and glory to Team CC.


Kubb isn't just a game to us—it's a way of life. Every wooden block we topple, every king we conquer, brings us closer to perfection. Join us on this epic journey!
